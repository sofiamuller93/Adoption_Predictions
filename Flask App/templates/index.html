ADOPT AN OLD PET

Older pets are less destructive than the younger guys. Their appearance, behavior and health are already established. They can learn new habits and commands.

ADOPT A PITBULL

Pitbulls are healthy dogs, they are easy to groom and they are very eager to please people.

ADOPT A BLACK CAT

Black cats are sleek, chic and dressed to impress. Wear black without taking a lint roller everywhere!
